Bhawani Bhikh Shukla

He was born on 15 June 1964 to Shri Ram Piyare Shukla and Shrimati Malti Devi in Vil. Baunapur, Gonda, Uttar Pradesh. He did not shown intrest in Studies. His father was four times Sarpanch of his village Baunapur. At very early age he lost his farther. After his father's death he contested for the post of Sarpanch of his village. He won with big margin of votes. He was married with Shrimati Jugrani.
