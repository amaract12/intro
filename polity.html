Political Career of Bhawani Bhikh Shukla

He is one of the greatest leaders of Katra Bazar Constituency. He firstly contested for Gram Panchayat election in 1983. He won that election and was elected as Sarpanch of Baunapur village after his father. In 2000 he was elected as Block Head(Pramukh) of Katra Bazar. In 2010 he contested in Birawa gram panchayat election and won. In 2015 he contested for District Panchayat Membership from Jamthara and he won with huge some of votes. In the same year his daughter Shrimati Bittu Devi and his sister Shrimati Danvati were elected as Sarpanch of Mahapara and Nadanwa gram panchayats, respectively. In 2017 UP VidhanSabha elections, he applied for ticket from Samajwadi Party but the ticket was given to ex-SP MLA of Katra Bazar Baijnath Dubey. In 2017 he joined BJP. And in 2021 his candidate Bhawani Prasad was the BJP supported winner for District Panchayat Membership from Katra Pratham. And he himself contested as Block Development Council member from Birawa First.
